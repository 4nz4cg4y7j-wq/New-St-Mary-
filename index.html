Welcome Home

Whether you're new to faith or looking for a church family, you belong here.

New St Mary Baptist Church is a warm, close-knit family serving the Ocala community. Our congregation is known for its friendly, welcoming spirit and messages that are direct, practical, and easy to apply to everyday life.

We invite you to worship with us, whether it's for a Sunday service, a special occasion, or simply to find a spiritual home.
